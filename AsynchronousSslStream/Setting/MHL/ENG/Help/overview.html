Overview

The software provides investors the ability to trade currencies, stocks and commodities by getting prices and placing orders in real-time over the Internet.

The software facilitates the investor:

  • to monitor real-time prices from the market leading price feed
  • to study the market trends through charting and analytical tools
  • to keep track of his/her orders and open positions with the real-time profit and loss
  • to view indicative account summary in real-time. If required, the investor is able to request a full account statement or ledger.

The platform has built-in encryption and security measures to ensure the communication between broker and investor is reasonably secure and safe.
