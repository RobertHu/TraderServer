Trading Panel

The Trading Panel contains quotes from market leading price feed. The Trading Panel occupies the at the top of the system's main screen.

    

The Trading Panel contains the following columns:

  • Item - shows the code of the trading instrument.  Investor may double mouse click the code for the placement of stop/limit order.
  • Bid - shows the current bidding price of the item.  Investor may double mouse click the bid price for the placement of DQ order.
  • Ask - shows the current asking price of the item.  Investor may double mouse click the ask price for the placement of DQ order.
  • Time - shows the time of the last price update.
  • Last - the last bid/ask prices of the instrument.
  • High - the highest price of the day.
  • Low - the lowest price of the day.
  • Open - the daily open price of the instrument
  • PrevClose - the last closing price of the instrument
  • Change - the net change between the current market price and the last closing price

When the prices are updating, the color of the price changes in the following manner:

  • Red - the price is up in comparison with the last update;
  • Blue - the price is down in comparison with the last update;
  • Black - no change.

Gray color represents the market of the item is close.

In addition to provide market price feeds to user, the Trading Panel enable user to place trading instruction through double mouse click at the Bid, Ask or the trading symbol.

  • To place order for spot
  • To place other order
