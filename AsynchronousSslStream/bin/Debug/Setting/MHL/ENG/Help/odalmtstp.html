To place a Limit/Stop Order

Please follow the steps below to complete a Limit/Stop Order.

1 Type - select LMT for Limit / Stop order

Limit Order processing dialog

2 Account - selection of trading account for the order
3 B/S - selection of Buy/Sell for the order
4 Price - enter the price to trigger the trade
5 Lot - enter the contract quantity for the trade
6 Balance - margin balance of the selected account
7 Equity - margin equity of the selected account
8 Close - enter the quantity corresponse to the open order if the trade is for liquidation purpose
9 OCO - button to enter an OCO order if the order is for liquidation purpose
10 Reset - button to reset the quantity entered for Close
11 Submit(F8) - button to submit the order to the dealing desk
12 Exit - button to exit the Trading Instruction dialogue

User will be able to verify the correctness of the order at the order Verification dialogue before it is finally placed to the Trading Desk.

When a Stop/Limit is successfully placed to the Trading Desk, the order bearing a reference code to signify its acceptance and a status of Unconfirm would be listed in the  Working Order panel. The Unconfirm status will stay as long as the order is not "Confirmed" for execution or "Cancelled" on whatever reasons.

Remarks:

  • Price entered for Limit/Stop order will be accepted only when it is certain points away from the current market price.
  • Stop order will be accepted only for hedging or liquidation purpose.
  • Execution of order will subject to the margin positions of the account at the time of execution
  • Order placed for Limit/Stop is good until the close of trading day.
    •

    Subject to the Trading rules of the company, the user may be possible to cancel a Limit/Stop order when it is in Unconfirm status. See How to cancell an unconfirmed order.
