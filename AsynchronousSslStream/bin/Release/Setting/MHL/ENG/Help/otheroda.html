To place other type of orders

The trading platform accepts the following trade types, the availability of it is subject to the company's policy and the custom and practice of the concerning market:

  • Limit/Stop (LMT)
  • One Cancel Other (OCO)
  • Market (MKT)
  • Market On Open (MOC) 
  • Market On Close (MOC)

To activate the order placing windows, double click the item code in the Trading Panel.
