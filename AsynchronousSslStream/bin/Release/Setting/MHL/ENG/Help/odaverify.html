Order verification

A Verification dialogue is available before submission of trading instruction to the Trading Desk. User is required to verify the following information:

1 Instrumnet - the instrument code for the trade.

Verification dialog

2 Trade details - the details of the trade, that includes the account, trade quantity, Buy/Sell, new or liquidation and opiton if it is Limit/Stop order.
3 Open Order - If the trade selected is for liquidation purpose, the description of its open order that includes the date, liquidating quantity and price.
4 Confirm (F8) - button to submit the order to the dealing desk
5 Cancel - button to cancel the trade and exit the Trading Instruction dialogue
