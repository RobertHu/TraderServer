Main Screen

The software's Main screen starts when the user has successfully logged on. The Main screen is the area where the user would carry most of his/her activities. The main screen consists of :

 

  • The Menu provides commands that the investor may need to operate.
  • The Trading Panel contains quotes from the leading market price feed. It is the area that the investor could place orders through mouse click the item code, Bid/Ask prices.
  • The Account Status contains real time margin positions of the trading account.
  • The Woking orders list is the area containing information of trading instructions that placed by the investor.
  • The Open orders list is the area containing information of contracts/orders that remain open in the market.
  • The Status Bar is the area containing the online status of the system
