To place an order for spot

The trading platform is designed and created to provide the ability to trade instantaneously from real time, streaming quotes. The current bid/ask prices appear in the Trading Panel shown below, and users can at any time click on the current bid or offer and instantaneously execute a trade. By offering instantaneous execution from live, two-way prices, users are always certain that the available prices are "at the market". Dealing from live quotes offers investors the advantage of anonymity and faster execution. Normal execution times are at a split of second.

Order for spot could be placed either through:

  • The Trading Panel
  • The Open Orders list

Placing order for spot at the Trading Panel

The Trading Panel provides view of live prices and operation for placing order. Users can at any time click on the current bid or ask price to place an order.

The Order placement dialogue will appear when the user double mouse clicks either at the Bid or Ask price of the desired item. 

1 The trading instrument code of the order
2 The trading account for the order
3 Enter the amount of contract to be traded
4 Live quote from the market
5 List of open buying contracts in the account
6 List of open selling contracts in the account
7 Enter the number of contract for liquidation
8 Buy / Sell button to trade on the price offer
9 Clear all quantity entered for liquidation purpose
10 Exit the Order Placement dialogue

Placing order for spot at the Open Order List

The order placed through the Open Order list is for liquidation purpose. If user wants to liquidate a certain order, simply check at the Close box and mouse click that order.

User may check multiple orders of the same instrument for liquidation at a single quote. When the order is clicked, the Liquidation Instruction dialogue will appear:

1 The trading instrument code of the order
2 Live quote from the market
3 The trading account of the selected order
4 The balance of the trading account of the selected order
5 The equity of the trading account of the selected order
6 The number of contract for liquidation, user may adjust the quantity at wish

When the user finds the quote acceptable, he may click the Submit button or F8 to complete the placement of the order. The Verification dialogue will appear as soon as the Buy/Sell button or Submit button is clicked.
